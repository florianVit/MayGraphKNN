Team Compatibility Analysis

How to Use This Tool

  • Select Candidate or Team depending on the type of analysis you want to perform.
  • Either choose from the dropdown list or manually enter a candidate or team name in the input field.
  • Click on Big 5 or Personality to generate the corresponding chart.
  • The result will appear as an image — click on it to enlarge and explore it in more detail (zoom & move).
  • If needed, use Clear Cache to reset stored data and reload fresh results.

This tool helps assess the compatibility of a candidate or a team based on their personality traits.
